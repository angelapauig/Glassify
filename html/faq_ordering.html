Ordering & Customization

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply log in to your account, go to the Products & Services page, customize your item using the 2D tool, and click "Add to Order." Follow the checkout process to confirm.

Can I upload my own plan?

Yes. During the ordering process, you will see an option to upload your blueprint or project file. This helps us process bulk or property-wide orders more accurately.

Do I need to create an account before ordering?

Yes, you must have an account to place an order. This allows us to save your design, send updates, and store your order history securely.

What if I need to order multiple items with different specifications?

You can add multiple customized items to your cart individually. Each one can have its own dimensions and specifications before finalizing your order.
