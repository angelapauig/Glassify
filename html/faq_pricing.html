Pricing & Quotation

How do I get a price for a custom order?

Use our online design tool for a real-time price estimate. For complex projects, you can upload your file or blueprint.

Are the prices final?

Prices on the website are estimates. The final price is confirmed at checkout and may vary with customization.

Do you offer bulk order discounts?

Yes. Contact our sales department to discuss special pricing for large orders.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. All charges, including taxes and shipping fees, are clearly itemized in your cart before you pay.

Is the quotation valid for a specific time?

Yes. Our quotations are valid for 30 days from the date of issue. After this period, prices may be subject to change.

What's the difference between a quote and an estimate?

An estimate is an approximate cost shown on the website. A quote is a formal, fixed price for your specific project, provided by our sales team.
