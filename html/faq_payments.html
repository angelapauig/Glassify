Payments

How can I pay for my order?

For online transactions on our website, we exclusively accept GCash. This allows for a fast and secure checkout process. For in-person payments at our physical location, we accept GCash, bank transfers, and all major credit and debit cards.

When will my payment be processed?

GCash payments are processed instantly. Bank transfers are confirmed upon receipt.

What if I have a payment issue?

If your payment fails, please double-check your details. If the problem persists, contact our support team with your order number.

Do I need to create an account before ordering?

Yes. Installment plans are available for in-person purchases at our location. You can choose from various terms depending on your bank and purchase amount. Please inquire with our staff for details.
