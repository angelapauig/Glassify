Shipping & Installation

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary based on your location and the complexity of your order. You will receive an estimated delivery date in your order confirmation email. We also offer expedited shipping options for an additional fee.

Can I track my order?

You can track your order directly on our website.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes. You can add installation during checkout. Our team will contact you to schedule it.

What if my product is damaged upon arrival?

Refuse the delivery and contact us immediately. If you've already accepted it, take photos and contact us within 48 hours to arrange a replacement.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

If you need to change your address, please contact us as soon as possible. We can update it as long as the order has not yet been shipped.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the Philippines. We are working on expanding our services in the future.
