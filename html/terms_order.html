Welcome to Glassify

1. Order Accuracy and Finalization


Please ensure that all product specifications—including dimensions, thickness, border type, and other details—are accurate before placing your order. Once the order is submitted, changes may not be accommodated unless approved by GlassWorth Builders.



2. Quotation and Pricing


An automated quotation is generated based on your selections. This serves as an estimated total and may be subject to adjustments based on final verification, availability of materials, or additional service requirements.



3. Payment Policy


You are required to choose your preferred payment option at the time of ordering. The following payment terms apply:

  • Option 1: Full Payment – Pay 100% of the total amount upon ordering.
  • Option 2: Down Payment – Pay 50% of the total amount as a non-refundable down payment. The remaining balance must be paid before delivery or upon completion, whichever is earlier.
  • Late payments beyond the agreed schedule may incur a 2% interest per week from the due date.


4. Order Processing and Lead Time


Orders will be processed only upon confirmation of payment. The estimated lead time for production and delivery is 5–14 working days, depending on order volume, complexity, and available stock.



5. Cancellations and Modifications


  • Cancellations are not allowed once production has begun.
  • Modifications to the order must be requested within 24 hours of placement and are subject to approval.
  • The down payment is non-refundable in the event of cancellation or failure to complete full payment.


6. Warranty and Claims


All products are covered by a limited warranty against factory defects. Damages caused by mishandling, wrong installation, or external factors after delivery are not covered. Warranty claims must be reported within 7 days of delivery.



7. Delivery and Pickup


  • You will be notified once your order is ready for delivery or pickup.
  • Additional fees may apply for delivery services depending on your location.
  • Delays due to force majeure or supply chain disruptions will be communicated promptly.


8. Privacy and Communication


Your personal and order information will only be used for processing and fulfilling your request. You agree to be contacted via email, SMS, or call regarding your order status and any follow-up.
